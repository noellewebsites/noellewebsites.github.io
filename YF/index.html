Active Kids = Healthy Kids!

Did You know?

Active kids are more likely to:
· Decrease the risk of catching the flu by 50%
· Have up to 40% higher test scores
· 15% more likely to go to college
· Have lower health costs
· Average 7-8% higher annual earnings as an adult
· Have reduced risk of heart disease, stroke, & diabetes
· Have decreased risk of cancer

We know how difficult it is right now for parents everywhere- whether homeschooling, virtual schooling, or sending your kids in-person! Youth Fitness is an excellent way to keep your kids active and healthy, while taking an hour out for yourself (cause let's be real... we all need that, sometimes)!

Taught by a licensed Youth Fitness specialist, your kids will get a well-rounded dose of exercise combined with activities that are sure help get the wiggles out and keep them social! This class is especially designed for kids age 5-11, and discounts are available for siblings. Join us for Youth Fitness and keep your kids healthy!
