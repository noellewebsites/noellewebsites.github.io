Active Older Adults (AOA)

Monday/Wednesday/
Friday
@ 10:00 AM



Taught by a multi-licensed professional, our AOA classes are specifically designed for adults who are looking to stay active, while taking care of their bodies and joints. AOA uses a combination of resistance tubes, weights, and chairs to improve functions of strength, balance, flexibility, & coordination. Movements are focused on overall mobility for decreased fall risk, and optimal skilled movement with daily activity.
