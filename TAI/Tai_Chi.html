TAi Chi

Tuesday/Thursday
@ 10:00 AM



“Tai chi is often described as "meditation in motion," but it might well be called "medication in motion." There is growing evidence that this mind-body practice, which originated in China as a martial art, has value in treating or preventing many health problems.”

-Harvard Medical School

Tai chi is a mind-body practice that involves a series of slow, flowing exercises that combine movement, meditation, and rhythmic breathing. Our experienced Tai Chi instructor will guide you through a series of mindful breathing and controlled movement techniques appropriate for all ages and fitness levels. Tai chi is used by practitioners around the World to enhance physical and mental health, as well as to improve posture, balance, flexibility, and strength. In addition, Tai Chi is said to boost mood, alleviate pain, decrease stress, strengthen the immune system, and improve heart health.
