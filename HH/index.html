What is HIIT-Hop?

HIIT-Hop is not a dance fitness class and not a bootcamp class. It is both. A combination of cardio, body-weight and plyometric movements and hip-hop dance. Every squat, every lunge, every burpee is driven by your favorite radio hits. Guaranteed to get your blood pumping, your heart rate up, and make you sweat and smile! Absolutely NO dance experience required!
