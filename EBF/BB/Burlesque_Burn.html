Turn down the lights, and turn up the heat! Joplin's only BURLESQUE class is giving new meaning to Body Positivity and Empowerment!

Burlesque Burn combines your body movement with the chair for a core workout that is all of the TEASE, without the strip. We will break down moves in a way that will make them easy to learn, while using your muscles in new ways to improve flexibility and strength. In addition to classes, you will be given access to a private page for dance breakdowns, special tutorials, and insight to exclusive events. You must be 17 or older to attend. You will need a clean pair of socks or non-grip dance shoes (not mandatory). Ladies only, please.
